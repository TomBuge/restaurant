THE ROCK INN
AT CHIDDINGSTONE
Welcome to The Rock, a timeless country pub nestled in the heart of Chiddingstone. Steeped in history and surrounded by rolling Kentish countryside, it is a place where good food and warm hospitality come together. ur kitchen celebrates the seasons, transforming locally sourced produce into comforting dishes full of flavour and heart. Settle in by the fire, raise a glass, and savour the charm of a true English inn. Every visit feels like coming home.
